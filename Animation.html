The Swanky Elephant

Elephants are my favorite animals in the world. They represent knowledge, power, and wisdom. They are often used as a spiritual symbol, as well as a religious icon. So, I decided to make an elephant swinging her trunk and her head falls off when you hover over her head because it shows that even the strongest of people sometimes lose themselves thinking over their head. And, her head also turns pink on the way down to show that with a little bit of love and support, everything will be okay.
